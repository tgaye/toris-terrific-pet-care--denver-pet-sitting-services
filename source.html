About Tori

Your Trusted Pet Care Professional

Hi, I'm Tori! I've been caring for animals my whole life and have turned my passion into a profession. As a Denver native, I understand the unique needs of pets in our mountain climate.

I'm certified in pet first aid and CPR, and have experience with all types of animals - from puppies and kittens to senior pets with special needs. Your pets' safety and happiness are my top priorities.
